Execuro — Eau de Parfum (For Men)

Bold. Confident. Refined. Execuro is crafted for the modern man who commands attention without saying a word. With its intense, masculine, and mature aroma, this fragrance captures the essence of power and sophistication, perfect for students, professionals, and achievers who strive to make a lasting impression. Designed for school, college, university, and office wear, Execuro delivers an aura of authority and elegance that stays with you throughout the day. Built with 30% pure perfume oil, it offers long-lasting performance, strong projection, and remarkable sillage, lasting 4–5 hours on skin and up to 10+ hours on clothes. Execuro isn’t just a fragrance, it’s a statement of ambition, focus, and success

Zephyr — Eau de Parfum (For Women)

Fresh. Powerful. Timeless. Deep Sea captures the essence of freedom and confidence, a fragrance made for those who dare to stand out. Deep Sea isn’t just a fragrance, it’s an attitude of confidence, depth, and unstoppable energy.
